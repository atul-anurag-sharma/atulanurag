## About Me

I am a Visiting Assistant Professor of Mathematics at [Ramapo College of New Jersey](https://www.ramapo.edu).

I received my PhD in Applied Mathematics (2025) from the Department of Mathematical Sciences at the New Jersey Institute of Technology, where I was advised by [Roy Goodman](https://roygoodman.net).

My research focuses on nonlinear dynamics, dynamical systems, and vortex motion.
