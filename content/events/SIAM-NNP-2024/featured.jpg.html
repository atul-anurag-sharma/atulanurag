Date and Location

The second meeting of SIAM-NNP will be held at Rochester Institute of Technology in Rochester, NY, November 1-3, 2024. 

Registration fees and travel costs

Registration fees will be set closer to the time of the event.

Registration for student presenters from NY, NJ, and PA will be free. PhD student presenters will be eligible to apply for travel reimbursement from the SIAM NNP Section.

Deadlines

Please submit talks, posters and minisymposia by Sep 6.

Minisymposia—A minisymposium will consist of five 20-minute presentations, with an additional five minutes for discussion after each presentation. Minisymposia organizers can submit up to two sessions per minisymposium, thus allowing up to 10 speakers.

Contributed Presentations—Either a poster or a contributed talk of 15 minutes + 5 for discussion.
