# Course title, summary, and position.
title: Math 110 PRECALCULUS
linktitle: Math
summary: A preparatory course for calculus with trigonometry. Topics include: absolute
values and inequalities; analytic geometry of conic sections; functions (linear, polynomial, rational, exponential, and logarithmic); as well as a study of plane trigonometry, including the trigonometric and inverse trigonometric functions, identities, equations, and formulas.
